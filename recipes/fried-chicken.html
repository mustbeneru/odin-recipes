Crispy Fried Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 Kilo Chicken (Legs / Thighs)
  • 1/4 cup Kalamansi Juice
  • 1/2 cup Soy Sauce
  • 3 cloves Garlic, peeled and minced
  • 1/2 tsp. Pepper
  • 1/2 tsp. Salt
  • 1/2 Cup Flour
  • 1/4 Corn Starch
  • Cooking Oil

How to cook?

  1. In a large bowl, combine Kalamansi Juice, Soy Sauce, Garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add Chicken and marinate for at least an hour or overnight.
  3. Drain Chicken from Marinade and let rest at room temperature for one hour.
  4. In a shallow dish, combine cornstarch and flour.
  5. Dredge Chicken in flour mixture to fully coat.
  6. In a wide, thick-bottomed pan over medium heat, heat about 2-inch deep of oil.
  7. Add chicken in batches and cook, turning on sides, until golden brown, crisp and cooked through. Remove from pan and drain on paper towels.
  8. Serve with Ketchup or homemade Gravy.
